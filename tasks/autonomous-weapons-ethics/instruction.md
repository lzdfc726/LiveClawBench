# Research Task

## Research Question

What are the major ethical frameworks and arguments surrounding autonomous weapon systems (AWS), and how do different nations and international bodies approach their regulation or prohibition?

## Your Task

Conduct deep, independent research on this topic using the browser and any available tools. Your goal is to produce a comprehensive, well-structured markdown report that thoroughly answers the research question.

## Report Requirements

1. **Comprehensiveness**: Cover the major theoretical frameworks, empirical evidence, and key perspectives related to the topic.
2. **Structure**: Organize the report with clear sections, subsections, and logical flow. Use markdown formatting (headers, lists, tables, code blocks) where appropriate.
3. **Sources**: Include a "Sources" section at the end of the report listing all references, URLs, and materials you consulted.
4. **Critical Analysis**: Go beyond summarization — evaluate the strengths and limitations of different approaches, and identify open questions or recent developments.
5. **Length**: The report should be substantive (at least 2000 words) to demonstrate thorough research.

## Output

Write your final report to: `~/.openclaw/output/report.md`
