# Corporate Tax Policy — VAT Filing Guide

## Current VAT Rates (Effective 2025-Q1 onwards)

| Category Code | Category Name | VAT Rate | Rate Type |
|---------------|--------------|----------|-----------|
| 5100 | Travel | 0% | Exempt |
| 5300 | Software & Subscriptions | 6% | Reduced |
| 5400 | Professional Services | 6% | Reduced |
| 5000 | Office Supplies | 13% | Standard |
| 5200 | Marketing & Advertising | 13% | Standard |
| 5500 | Utilities | 13% | Standard |
| 5600 | Meals & Entertainment | 13% | Standard |
| 5700 | Transport | 13% | Standard |
| 5800 | Insurance | 13% | Standard |
| 5900 | Miscellaneous | 13% | Standard |

### Rate Calculation

For each invoice line item:
- VAT amount = line item amount x applicable VAT rate
- Total VAT for invoice = sum of VAT amounts for all line items

## Historical Rates

Prior to 2025-Q1, the standard rate was 20% and the reduced rate was 10%. All filings from 2025-Q1 onward use the current rates above.

## Special Exemptions

1. **Government Entities**: Services provided to government entities (vendor code starting with 'GOV-') are exempt from VAT. This applies to the full invoice amount regardless of category code.

2. **International Travel**: Travel expenses for international trips are subject to the reverse charge mechanism. The VAT is not calculated by the company but must be reported separately on the reverse charge line of the VAT return.

3. **Zero-rated Items**: Category 5100 (Travel) is zero-rated for domestic travel. No VAT is calculated on these items.

## Filing Requirements

| Quarter | Period | Due Date |
|---------|--------|----------|
| Q1 | January - March | April 15 |
| Q2 | April - June | July 15 |
| Q3 | July - September | October 15 |
| Q4 | October - December | January 15 |

### Filing Process

1. Identify all invoices for the quarter by invoice date
2. Calculate VAT for each invoice based on the rates above
3. Sum all VAT amounts to determine total quarterly liability
4. Submit the VAT return to the tax authority
5. Update this policy with the filing results
