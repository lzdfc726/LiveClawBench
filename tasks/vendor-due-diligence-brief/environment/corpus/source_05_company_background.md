# CloudEdge Systems — Company Background

**Founded:** 2022, San Francisco, CA
**Employees:** 48 (as of Q1 2026)
**Funding:** Series A — $18M raised in August 2023, led by Ironwood Ventures

## Leadership

- **CEO:** Diana Soto — previously Director of Engineering at Splunk (2018–2022)
- **CTO:** Raj Mehta — formerly Principal Engineer at HashiCorp (2019–2022)
- **VP Sales:** Terence Bouchard — background in enterprise SaaS sales (Salesforce, 2016–2022)

## Products

DataGuard (launched Q3 2023) is the company's sole product. There is no current roadmap for a second product line. The company has not yet reached profitability; current runway estimated at 18–24 months on existing capital.

## Market Position

CloudEdge competes primarily against incumbent solutions from HashiCorp Vault Enterprise, AWS Macie, and Google Cloud DLP. The DataGuard value proposition is a simpler integration path at a lower price point than enterprise offerings from the major cloud vendors.

## Customer Base

As of April 2026, CloudEdge has 14 enterprise customers. Industries served: healthcare (4), financial services (3), SaaS (5), government (2).
