# Security Questionnaire — CloudEdge Systems Responses

*Completed by: Rena Kolawole, Head of Information Security, CloudEdge Systems*
*Date: March 2026*

## Certifications & Compliance

**Q: Does CloudEdge Systems hold current SOC 2 Type II certification?**
A: CloudEdge Systems completed a SOC 2 Type I assessment in October 2025. Our SOC 2 Type II audit is currently in progress, expected completion Q4 2026. We can provide the Type I report upon request under NDA.

**Q: Is CloudEdge GDPR compliant?**
A: Yes. We have appointed a Data Protection Officer and maintain a Record of Processing Activities (RoPA). EU data residency options are available via our Frankfurt region deployment.

**Q: Does CloudEdge perform annual penetration testing?**
A: We conducted an internal penetration test in January 2026. Third-party pen testing is scheduled for Q3 2026 — we have not yet completed an external third-party assessment.

## Vulnerability Management

**Q: How are security vulnerabilities tracked and remediated?**
A: We use a private GitHub Security Advisory workflow. Critical vulnerabilities are patched within 72 hours of detection. Medium severity patches are released in the next monthly update cycle.

**Q: What is your mean time to remediate (MTTR) for critical CVEs?**
A: Our current MTTR for critical CVEs is 4.2 days (measured over the past 6 months). We are targeting a 48-hour MTTR by end of 2026.
