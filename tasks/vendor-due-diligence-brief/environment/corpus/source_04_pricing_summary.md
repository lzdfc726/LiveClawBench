# CloudEdge Systems — Pricing Summary (Enterprise Tier)

*Confidential — For Evaluation Purposes Only*

## Base License

| Tier | Monthly Fee | Included Data Volume | Nodes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Starter | $4,500/mo | Up to 500 GB/month | Up to 5 |
| Professional | $12,000/mo | Up to 5 TB/month | Up to 20 |
| Enterprise | $28,000/mo | Up to 50 TB/month | Unlimited |

## Overage Fees

Data processed beyond the included volume is billed at $0.08/GB for the Professional tier and $0.05/GB for the Enterprise tier. Overage fees are calculated at the end of each billing cycle and invoiced separately. There is no cap on overage charges.

## Add-On Services

| Add-On | Price |
|---|---|
| Dedicated customer success manager | +$2,500/mo |
| On-premise key management (HSM) | +$5,000/mo |
| 24/7 premium support upgrade | +$3,000/mo |
| Additional EU data residency nodes | +$1,800/mo per node |

## Contract Terms

- Minimum 12-month commitment on all tiers
- Annual prepayment discount: 10%
- Early termination fee: 3 months of base license fee
- Price lock: 24 months from contract start; after that, price adjustments subject to up to 8% annual increase
