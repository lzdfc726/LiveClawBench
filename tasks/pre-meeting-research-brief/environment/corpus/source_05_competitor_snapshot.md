# Competitor Landscape — NovaTech vs. Alternatives

**Primary competitors to NovaTech:**

1. **DataForge** — Strong in feature stores but weak in pipeline orchestration. Recently lost two Fortune 500 clients to NovaTech. Pricing is 20% higher for comparable compute.

2. **Streamline AI** — Open-source core with enterprise support tier. Faster iteration on community features but slower enterprise roadmap. Not compliant with FedRAMP (limiting US government sales).

3. **PipelineOS** — Backed by a major cloud vendor. Tight integration with their cloud stack, but customers report vendor lock-in concerns.

**NovaTech's differentiation:** Strong compliance tooling (DataShield), neutral cloud position, and a faster enterprise onboarding process (average 6 weeks vs. 14-week industry average).
