# Partnership Context — NovaTech AI

Our previous conversations with NovaTech began in October 2025 when we explored potential integrations between our data platform and their NovaScale ML orchestration layer.

**Key concern raised by NovaTech's legal team:** Data residency and processor obligations under GDPR Article 28. Any joint pipeline processing EU customer data must have a signed Data Processing Agreement (DPA) in place before go-live. This is non-negotiable per NovaTech's compliance posture.

**Our leverage:** We have an existing DPA framework that has been approved by our EU Data Protection Officer. NovaTech is familiar with this framework from a prior engagement with one of our subsidiaries.

**Partnership model under discussion:** A co-sell arrangement where we embed NovaTech's DataShield module into our offering, and NovaTech lists our platform as a preferred data source in their marketplace.
