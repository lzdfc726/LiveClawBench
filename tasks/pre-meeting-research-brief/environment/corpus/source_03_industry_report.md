# Enterprise AI Platform Market — Q1 2026 Snapshot
*Source: Meridian Research Group*

The enterprise AI platform market reached $18.2B in 2025, growing 34% year-over-year. Three segments dominate: ML orchestration, feature stores, and model registries.

**Key trends:**
- Regulatory pressure (EU AI Act, GDPR enforcement) is forcing vendors to invest in compliance tooling.
- Hybrid cloud demand is accelerating; 62% of enterprise workloads run across at least two clouds.
- Vendor consolidation: three major acquisitions in 2025 reduced the competitive field from 40 players to ~28.

**Outlook:** Analysts project continued 25–30% annual growth through 2027, driven by generative AI adoption in enterprise workflows.
