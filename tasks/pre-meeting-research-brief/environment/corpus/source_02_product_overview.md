# NovaTech AI — Product Overview

NovaTech's flagship offering is the **NovaScale platform**, a cloud-native solution for orchestrating ML pipelines across heterogeneous infrastructure. It supports on-premise, hybrid, and multi-cloud deployment.

**Scale:** As of Q1 2026, the platform serves over 500 enterprise clients, processing an average of 2.4 billion inference requests per day.

**Key modules:**
- PipelineForge: drag-and-drop ML workflow builder
- DataShield: automated PII detection and masking
- ObserveAI: real-time model drift detection and alerting

**Pricing model:** Subscription-based, tiered by compute units consumed. Enterprise tier includes dedicated SLAs and on-premise deployment options.
