# NovaTech AI — Company Profile

NovaTech AI is an enterprise AI platform company founded in 2021 and headquartered in San Francisco, CA. The company focuses on ML workflow automation and data pipeline orchestration for large enterprises.

**Funding:** NovaTech raised a $85M Series B in late 2023, led by Horizon Ventures. Total funding to date is approximately $120M.

**Team:** The company has grown to 130 employees. CEO is Alex Morgan, previously VP Engineering at DataStream Corp. CTO is Priya Nair, a veteran of Google Brain.

**Customers:** NovaTech primarily targets Fortune 500 companies and fast-growing tech firms.
