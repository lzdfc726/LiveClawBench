Open the Chat App at http://localhost:5003/chat.

In the chat, you will see several channels with messages. Some messages are asking for specific stickers:
- In the #pets channel, someone asked for a cat sticker.
- In the #space channel, someone asked for a rocket sticker.

Your tasks:
1. Read the messages in #pets and #space to understand what stickers are needed.
2. Go to the Sticker Store and acquire the sticker packs that contain the requested stickers.
3. Return to the chat and send the appropriate sticker in each channel as a reply.
4. Open the Sticker Manager and move all cat stickers to the "Favorite" category.
