# Basel III Stress Testing Framework Overview

## Introduction

This document introduces stress testing methods for traditional banking systems and has no direct relevance to DeFi.

## Stress Testing Types

### 1. Credit Risk Stress Testing
Assesses loan portfolio performance under recession scenarios.

### 2. Market Risk Stress Testing
Assesses trading account losses under extreme market volatility.

### 3. Liquidity Stress Testing
Assesses bank liquidity conditions under deposit run scenarios.

## Key Indicators
- CET1 ratio
- Leverage ratio
- Liquidity Coverage Ratio (LCR)
- Net Stable Funding Ratio (NSFR)

## Conclusion

The traditional banking industry's regulatory framework is mature but complex. This report serves only as general background reference for financial risk management.
